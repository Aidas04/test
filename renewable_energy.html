Renewable Energy


What is Renewable Energy?

Renewable energy is a form of energy that can replenish over time and can be reused naturally. Solar energy comes from the sun. Wind energy comes from the wind. Hydro energy comes from the water. The way in which humans collect this energy all varies based on the energy. Solar panels can harness the energy of the sun. Wind turbines can harness energy from the wind. Hydro dams can harness energy from flowing water. Each energy type is naturally found on the planet therefore it is classified as a renewable energy. Although these methods are used to harness the energy, a large population still choose to use non-renewable energy such as fossil fuels that cannot be reused and harm the planet.
